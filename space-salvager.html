HOW TO PLAY

GREEN / YELLOW = CORECYAN = PLAYERPINK = ENEMYRED = LASERORANGE = ASTEROIDVIOLET = UPGRADE

RECOVER

ENERGY COREGreen-yellow orbs score. Every five clean pickups raises your multiplier.
ASTEROIDOrange means breakable. Dash through a large one and its fast fragments can smash enemies or trigger lasers.
LASERRed targeting rigs explode after a short fuse. Their blast damages enemies and asteroids too.

ENEMIES

SEEKER DRONETracks your ship while steering around large asteroids.
STRAFER DRONEOrbits and fires pink bolts. Its shots can break asteroids.
CHARGER DRONEStops, paints a pink attack lane, then lunges. Dash across the lane.

POWER-UPS

PHASE SHIELDA violet hull glow means contact immunity; enemies and asteroids are pushed away.
HULL REPAIRRestores one damaged hull segment; it will not spawn at full hull.
OVERDRIVEViolet system boost for speed, instant handling, hard braking, and pulse recharge.
MAGNET SURGEGreatly expands and strengthens core pull for 12 seconds.

CONTROLS & CHAINS

STEERWASD or arrow keys. The ship snaps toward input and brakes quickly.
PULSE / CHAIN BLASTSpace hits nearby enemies. Chain Blast jumps outward from enemies already hit; dashed lines show each jump.
DASHC or Shift launches a fast swipe. The grey short flame means dash is still charging.
SHARD CANNONClaim the boss blueprint, then press F or X to fire a cyan bolt every 1.3 seconds. It can crack asteroids into boss-killing fragments.
